finplan
=======

This is the beginning of a new project. 

I use applications to help manage and track my finances but they don't provide a good way to model the interaction between accounts that I have. For example, my wife and I both have an income that gets deposited to our individual accounts on a regular basis. From there the money may travel into other accounts that we share for purposes of saving or paying bills, or we may put the money into our own IRAs or other investment accounts or pay off balances on credit cards. 

1. Keep track of relationships between accounts (money in, money out, regularly scheduled transfers, paychecks, bill payments, etc.)
2. Model account state into the future. Run simulations.
3. All data will be input manually. No external connections to financial institutions.
